# IPO-004 manual extraction MVP design

The accepted design is documented in
[`docs/architecture/ipo-004-manual-extraction-mvp.md`](../../architecture/ipo-004-manual-extraction-mvp.md).

Key decisions are immutable normalized revisions, exact reported-unit retention,
one verified cached DRHP/RHP per revision, complete page-level provenance,
admin-only access, and a typed raw-data bridge without automatic factor scoring.
